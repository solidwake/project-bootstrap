Frequently asked Questions

There are 3 Gundam Pilot Academy locations to meet the needs of prospective attendees. Our main location is at Side 6 in the Baldur Bay colony, There are also locations on Earth and the moon, at Jaburo and Von Braun, respectively.

Admission is 100,000 Gira for the entire program. This cost covers room and board, uniform, and any supplies you may need. Prospective attendees may seek sponsorship. Payment plans are also available.

Prospective attendees will need at the least, experience piloting a Ball of any type. Any mobile suit engineering knowledge/experience is a plus.
